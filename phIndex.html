Collaborative Management of Logistics

At Collogistics we foster collaboration between our members (or collaborators as we like to call them). We help them identify logistical hurdles (or barriers) that might "get in their way". Once these hurdles are identified we do all that we can to boost our Collaborators so that they can jump over the hurdles! It's as simple as that.

One type of logistical hurdle that needs to be overcome occurs through chronic illness. There are many deseases that our Collaborative members have to endure. As we continue to grow our site we plan to identify more deseases (logistical hurdles) that confront disparate Collaborators and help them leap hurdles.

There exists a wide range of logistical hurdles for a wide range of Collaborators. Using the menu choices (see left panel) you can select from certain areas of Personal Health concerns. If areas that concern you are missing please contact us and we will consider helping you jump your own specific hurdles.

Example areas of concern that we now offer management of logistics include:

  • Collaborative Tracking of Glucose Levels (for diabetes patients)
  • Collaborative Meal Planning (for folks with dementia)

We expect the above list to grow as we garner suggestions from users who find that whatever process or endeavor they are trying to accomplish they are impeded by logistical hurdles that require better management!

Our Mission

Collogistics provides a capable repository of professionals as well as free or licensed software. We promote the Collaborative Model via our services and software specifically geared for helping ordinary folks overcome nuisance logistics.

Part of our role is to educate and orient site users to show them how to become more efficient due to inherent benefits accrued via a team approach and collaboration.

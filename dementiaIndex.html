Collaborative Management of Logistics

Folks with some form of dementia, such as Alzheimer's Disease, are advised to carefully keep written notes including upcomming events (calendar entries). This simple discipline helps to maintain schedules and avoids forgotten appointments. There exists several fine computer applications (or mobile phone Apps) to help with calendar-specific logistics.

Some everyday events, such as plans for meals, constitute logistics that can get in the way of an everyday calendar application. What's needed is a specifc meal-planner application or App. We have developed this. It's never too late to share your ideas with regards how this might work for forgetful folks. This is because the web app is in "beta" (or test). Please contact us with your ideas that would enhance the beta!

To access the working beta simply use your iPad browser and visit the site: PlateSlate
